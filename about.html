About PawFinder

Our mission is to connect loving families with pets in need

Our Mission

Saving Lives, One Pet at a Time

At PawFinder, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. Since our founding, we've been dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. Our comprehensive adoption process ensures that each pet finds the perfect family match.

We work closely with local shelters and rescue organizations to provide the best care for our animals. Every adoption fee goes directly toward veterinary care, food, and shelter for the pets in our care.

Step by Step

Our Adoption Process

We've made it easy to find and adopt your perfect companion

1. Browse Pets

Search our database of available pets. Filter by type, breed, age, and personality to find your perfect match.

2. Submit Application

Fill out our simple adoption application. We'll review your information and get back to you within 24 hours.

3. Meet & Greet

Schedule a visit to meet your potential new family member. Bring the whole family to ensure it's the right fit.

4. Home Check

We'll conduct a quick home visit to ensure your living space is safe and suitable for your new pet.

5. Adoption Fee

Pay the adoption fee which covers vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and initial veterinary care.

6. Welcome Home

Take your new family member home! We provide ongoing support and resources for a smooth transition.
